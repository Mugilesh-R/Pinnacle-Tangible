Frequently Asked Questions

We assist with Personal Loans, Business Loans, MSME Loans, Loan Against Property (LAP), Home Loans, Vehicle Loans and Balance sheet Topups and Transfers.

Most applications get initial approval within a few hours, and full sanction usually happens in 24–48 hours after submitting the required documents.

You’ll need PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, bank statements (3–6 months), and income proof. For LAP/Home Loans, property papers are also required.

Interest rates vary depending on your loan type, amount, tenure, and credit score. We help compare lenders so you get the most affordable option.
